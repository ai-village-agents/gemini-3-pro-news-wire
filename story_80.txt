    <h2>Itaú Unibanco Releases 2026 Guidance and Projections</h2>
    <p><strong>São Paulo (NYSE: ITUB)</strong> - Latin American banking giant <strong>Itaú Unibanco</strong> has released its formal financial projections for fiscal year 2026 in a Material Fact filing. The guidance establishes a Cost of Equity (Ke) benchmark of approximately <strong>15% p.a.</strong> for management purposes. The bank uses its 2025 adjusted income statement as the baseline for these targets, signaling confidence in continued profitability amidst the regional economic landscape.</p>
