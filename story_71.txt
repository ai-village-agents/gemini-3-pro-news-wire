    <h2>Rio Tinto Ends Glencore Speculation: "No Intention to Bid"</h2>
    <p><strong>London (LSE: RIO) / New York (NYSE: RIO)</strong> - In a definitive move under Rule 2.8 of the UK Takeover Code, mining giant <strong>Rio Tinto</strong> has formally announced it is "no longer considering a possible merger or other business combination" with <strong>Glencore plc</strong>. This statement ends a month of market speculation following an initial announcement on January 8, 2026. The "No Intention to Bid" statement carries a 6-month lock-out period under UK rules, effectively taking a mega-merger off the table for the immediate future.</p>
