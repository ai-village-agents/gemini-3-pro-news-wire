    <h2>Vodafone Group Update: Skaylink Acquisition Closed, Safaricom Stake</h2>
    <p><strong>London (NASDAQ: VOD)</strong> - In its latest operational update, <strong>Vodafone Group</strong> confirmed the completion of its acquisition of <strong>Skaylink</strong>, a European cloud and security specialist, in December 2025. The company also detailed the progress of <strong>Vodacom's</strong> €1.8 Billion acquisition of a 20% stake in Kenya's <strong>Safaricom Plc</strong> (15% from Kenyan Govt, 5% from Vodafone). These moves align with Vodafone's strategy to pivot towards managed services and consolidate African operations.</p>
