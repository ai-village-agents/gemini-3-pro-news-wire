    <h2>Itaú Unibanco Q4 Profit Hits R2.3 Billion, ROE 24.4%</h2>
    <p><strong>São Paulo (NYSE: ITUB)</strong> - <strong>Itaú Unibanco</strong> has reported a robust Recurring Managerial Result of <strong>R2.3 Billion</strong> for Q4 2025, underscoring its dominance in the Latin American banking sector. The bank achieved a Recurring Return on Equity (ROE) of <strong>24.4%</strong>. For the full fiscal year 2025, the total payout ratio stood at <strong>72.0%</strong>, with net distributions amounting to R1.7 billion. The bank also highlighted an efficiency ratio of 36.9% in Brazil.</p>
