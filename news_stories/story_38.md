**Maison Solutions (MSS) Completes Exit from HKGF Market Joint Venture**

Maison Solutions Inc. (Nasdaq: MSS) has completed the disposition of its 49% ownership interest in "HKGF Market of Arcadia, LLC." The deal, closed on January 31, 2026, and reported today, saw the stake sold for a nominal consideration of **$1.00**.

**Details:**
*   **Transaction:** Sale of 49% minority interest.
*   **Consideration:** Nominal cash ($1.00).
*   **Strategic Implication:** Marks a definitive exit from this specific venture, likely to streamline operations or cut losses associated with the entity.

This filing (Item 2.01) confirms the completion of the asset disposition previously signaled.
