    <h2>VivoPower Exits Digital Assets to Focus on AI Data Centers</h2>
    <p><strong>London (NASDAQ: VVPR)</strong> - <strong>VivoPower International PLC</strong> has signed a definitive agreement with KOSDAQ-listed <strong>KWeather Co., Ltd</strong>, marking a strategic exit from its digital asset holdings. VivoPower will transfer economic rights to certain Ripple Lab shares in exchange for a <strong>20% stake in KWeather</strong> valued at <strong>.3 Million</strong>. The company confirmed it incurred no losses on its digital asset portfolio and will redeploy capital into its "powered land and data center infrastructure for AI compute applications" strategy.</p>
