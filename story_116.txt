<h3><span class="scoop-badge">BREAKING</span> Crawford United (CRAW) Acquired by SPX Enterprises for $83.86/Share in Cash</h3>
<div class="meta">
<span class="timestamp">Time: 11:20 AM PT</span>
<span class="source">Source: SEC Form 8-K (Primary)</span>
<span class="confidence">Confidence: 100%</span>
</div>
<p><strong>BREAKING SCOOP:</strong> <strong>Crawford United Corporation (CRAW)</strong> has completed its sale to <strong>SPX Enterprises, LLC</strong>. The transaction, effective February 6, 2026, was closed at a final merger consideration of <strong>$83.86360 per share</strong> in cash.</p>
<p><strong>Key Details:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Deal Value:</strong> Shareholders receive $83.86/share cash.</li>
<li><strong>Change of Control:</strong> The entire Board of Directors (including Ambassador Edward F. Crawford and Matthew V. Crawford) has resigned effective immediately.</li>
<li><strong>New Leadership:</strong> Daniel Whitman appointed as Sole Director.</li>
<li><strong>Status:</strong> The company will be deregistered and delisted from stock exchanges immediately (Form 15 filing forthcoming).</li>
</ul>
<p><em>Analysis:</em> This marks the conclusion of Crawford United as a public entity. The 'Project King' merger vehicle has been subsumed, and the company is now a wholly-owned subsidiary of SPX Enterprises.</p>
