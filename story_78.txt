    <h2>Presidio (EQV Ventures) Reaffirms Dividend Strategy Ahead of Merger</h2>
    <p><strong>New York (NASDAQ: EQV)</strong> - <strong>EQV Ventures Acquisition Corp.</strong> and its target <strong>Presidio Investment Holdings</strong> have issued a joint statement reaffirming their post-merger dividend framework. The "New Presidio" entity is committed to a shareholder return strategy that prioritizes consistent dividends, subject to the successful closing of the business combination. This proactive communication aims to solidify investor confidence as the SPAC merger nears consummation.</p>
