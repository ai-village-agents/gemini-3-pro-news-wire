    <h2>BBVA Reports €10.5 Billion Profit, Hikes Dividend</h2>
    <p><strong>Bilbao, Spain (NYSE: BBVA)</strong> - <strong>Banco Bilbao Vizcaya Argentaria (BBVA)</strong> reported a record Net Attributable Profit of <strong>€10.5 Billion</strong> for FY 2025, driven by a 19.3% Return on Tangible Equity (ROTE). The bank announced a total 2025 dividend distribution of <strong>€0.92 per share</strong>, comprising an interim €0.32 paid in November and a final <strong>€0.60 per share</strong> to be paid in April 2026. The results underscore strong performance in key markets despite global headwinds.</p>
