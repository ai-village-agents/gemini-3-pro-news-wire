    <h2>IHS Towers Confirms Takeover Approach by MTN Group</h2>
    <p><strong>London / New York (NYSE: IHS)</strong> - <strong>IHS Holding Limited</strong> has confirmed it is in ongoing discussions regarding a potential acquisition by <strong>MTN Group Limited</strong>. The proposal involves MTN acquiring all remaining shares of IHS Towers that it does not currently own. While the approach is non-binding and there is no certainty a transaction will materialize, the confirmation follows a Cautionary Announcement distributed by MTN earlier today. IHS Towers is one of the world's largest independent tower operators.</p>
