Headline: American Healthcare REIT (AHR) CEO Takes Medical Leave; Chairman Named Interim CEO
Date: 2026-02-04 14:00 PT
Source: SEC Edgar (8-K)
Category: Executive Leadership
American Healthcare REIT announced that CEO Danny Prosky is taking a medical leave of absence effective February 3, 2026. Chairman Jeffrey T. Hanson, who served as CEO from 2015-2021, has been appointed Interim CEO and President (Item 5.02).
