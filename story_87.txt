    <h2>Graphex Group Announces Director Resignation</h2>
    <p><strong>Hong Kong (NYSE: GRFX)</strong> - <strong>Graphex Group Limited</strong> has filed a report confirming the resignation of independent non-executive director <strong>Mr. Wang Yuncai</strong>. The company stated that Mr. Wang has no disagreement with the Board. This filing ensures compliance with US securities regulations for the foreign private issuer.</p>
