    <h2>Vista Credit Strategic Lending Declares January Distribution</h2>
    <p><strong>New York (NASDAQ: VISTA)</strong> - <strong>Vista Credit Strategic Lending Corp.</strong> has announced its January 2026 monthly distribution. Class I shareholders will receive a net distribution of <strong>/bin/bash.16000 per share</strong>, while Class S shareholders will receive <strong>/bin/bash.14574 per share</strong> (net of fees). The distribution is payable on February 10, 2026, to holders of record as of January 30. The company confirmed it has no Class D shares outstanding.</p>
