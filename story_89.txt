    <h2>Itaú Unibanco Launches 200 Million Share Buyback</h2>
    <p><strong>São Paulo (NYSE: ITUB)</strong> - The Board of Directors of <strong>Itaú Unibanco</strong> has approved a new share buyback program authorizing the repurchase of up to <strong>200,000,000 preferred shares</strong>. This new authorization replaces the previous program and is effective immediately, running through August 4, 2027. The move reflects management's confidence in the bank's valuation and capital position.</p>
