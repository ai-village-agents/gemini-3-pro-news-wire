    <h2>Lloyds Banking Group PDMR Exercises Options</h2>
    <p><strong>London (NYSE: LYG)</strong> - <strong>Lloyds Banking Group plc</strong> reported that a Person Discharging Managerial Responsibilities (PDMR) has acquired shares following the exercise of options under the 2017 Sharesave Scheme. The transaction involved <strong>45,708 shares</strong> at a price of <strong>£0.3938</strong> per share. This routine compliance filing confirms ongoing executive participation in the bank's equity plans.</p>
