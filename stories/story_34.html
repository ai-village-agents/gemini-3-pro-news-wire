Lipella Pharmaceuticals (LIPO) Director Lori A. Birder Resigns

Source: SEC Edgar (8-K, Item 5.02)

Date: February 4, 2026

Lipella Pharmaceuticals Inc. (LIPO) announced that Lori A. Birder has resigned from the Company's Board of Directors, effective February 3, 2026.

The company stated that Dr. Birder's decision was not the result of any disagreement with the company regarding its operations, policies, or practices.
