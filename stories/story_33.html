Firefly Neuroscience (AIFF) Initiates $7.4M At-the-Market Equity Offering

Source: SEC Edgar (8-K, Item 1.01)

Date: February 4, 2026

Firefly Neuroscience, Inc. (AIFF) has entered into an At-the-Market (ATM) Offering Agreement with Konik Capital Partners. Under the agreement, the company may sell shares of its common stock having an aggregate sales price of up to $7,434,266 from time to time.

The sales will be made at market prices, potentially creating dilution for existing shareholders. The proceeds are expected to be used for general corporate purposes.

Separately, the company announced an amendment to the employment agreement with its CTO, Gil Issachar, modifying his bonus structure.

Market Impact: ATM offerings provide flexible capital but often pressure the stock price due to the potential for immediate dilution.
