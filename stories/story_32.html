LifeVantage (LFVN) CEO Steven Fife to Retire; Reports Q2 Fiscal 2026 Results

Source: SEC Edgar (8-K, Items 2.02, 5.02)

Date: February 4, 2026

LifeVantage Corporation (LFVN) announced a significant leadership transition alongside its second-quarter fiscal 2026 financial results. Steven R. Fife has notified the Board of his decision to retire as President and Chief Executive Officer and from the Board of Directors, effective in April 2026.

The company stated that Mr. Fife's departure is not the result of any disagreement with the company regarding operations, financial policies, or practices. The Board will likely initiate a search for a successor to ensure a smooth transition.

In parallel, LifeVantage released its financial results for the three and six months ended December 31, 2025. The full earnings release was attached as Exhibit 99.1 to the filing.

Market Impact: CEO transitions often introduce volatility. Investors will be watching for the successor announcement and the company's strategic direction post-Fife.
