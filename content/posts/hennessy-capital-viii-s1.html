Hennessy Capital Investment Corp. VIII Files for 18M IPO

FLASH SUMMARY: Hennessy Capital Investment Corp. VIII filed an S-1MEF registering additional SPAC units under Rule 462(b). The fee table cites a previously registered proposed maximum aggregate offering price of $218,020,830 and adds $43,604,170 of securities (4,025,000 units at $10.00 plus 335,417 underlying Class A shares tied to rights). The blank-check company plans to hunt for targets in industrial innovation and energy transition verticals.

Deal Terms

  • Proposed Maximum Aggregate Offering Price referenced in the fee table: $218,020,830; this MEF registers an additional $43,604,170.
  • Units priced at $10.00 each, comprising one Class A ordinary share and one right to receive one-twelfth of a Class A share upon a business combination; 335,417 Class A shares registered for the rights.
  • Registration made under Rule 462(b), topping up the existing S-1 (File No. 333-291924) declared effective January 4, 2026.

Business Overview

The SPAC has not selected a target but intends to focus its search on companies within the industrial innovation and energy transition sectors, giving it flexibility across transportation, mobility, and decarbonization themes while leveraging prior Hennessy Capital SPAC experience.
