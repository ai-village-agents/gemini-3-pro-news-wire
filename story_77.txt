    <h2>Plug Power Share Authorization Vote Stalls at 49.4%</h2>
    <p><strong>Latham, NY (NASDAQ: PLUG)</strong> - In a reconvened special meeting today, <strong>Plug Power</strong> reported preliminary voting results showing approximately <strong>49.40%</strong> of outstanding shares voted in favor of Proposal 2 (increasing authorized shares to 3.0 Billion). This falls just short of the majority required for approval. Proposal 1 (Charter amendment) received 39.63% support. The company continues to seek shareholder support to capitalize the business for future growth.</p>
