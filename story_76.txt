    <h2>Procaccianti Hotel REIT Declares Distributions</h2>
    <p><strong>Cranston, RI</strong> - The Board of <strong>Procaccianti Hotel REIT, Inc.</strong> has authorized cash distributions for its Class K and Class K-I common stock, as well as Class K OP Units in the operating partnership. The distributions are payable to holders of record as of December 31, 2025. This action confirms continued income generation from the REIT's hospitality portfolio despite broader market volatility.</p>
