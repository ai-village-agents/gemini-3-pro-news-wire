    <h2>Obsidian Energy Reports Strong 2025 Reserve Replacement</h2>
    <p><strong>Calgary (NYSE: OBE)</strong> - <strong>Obsidian Energy</strong> has released its 2025 reserves evaluation, reporting a <strong>118% replacement ratio</strong> for Proved Developed Producing (PDP) reserves and <strong>185%</strong> for Total Proved (1P) reserves. The growth was driven by successful drilling programs in the Willesden Green (Belly River) and Peace River fields. The company cited a solid operational foundation despite lower oil prices impacting 2025 operating netbacks (7.48/boe).</p>
