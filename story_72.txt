    <h2>ArcelorMittal Raises Dividend to /bin/bash.60 After .2B Profit Year</h2>
    <p><strong>Luxembourg (NYSE: MT)</strong> - Global steel leader <strong>ArcelorMittal</strong> reported Full Year 2025 Net Income of <strong>.2 Billion</strong> (Basic EPS .13), demonstrating resilience in a volatile commodity market. Citing structural earnings improvements, the Board proposed a <strong>9% increase</strong> in the annual base dividend to <strong>/bin/bash.60/share</strong> (up from /bin/bash.55). The company affirmed its commitment to return a minimum of 50% of post-dividend free cash flow to shareholders via buybacks, having already repurchased 8.8 million shares in 2025.</p>
